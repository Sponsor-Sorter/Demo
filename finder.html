    Sponsor Sorter Finder Help:
    Use this page to search for creators/influencers, browse open sponsorship offers, or find groups that accept offers.
    • User Search: Find creators/influencers by name, content type, location, or platform.
    • Offer Search: Browse and apply for active public sponsorship offers.
    • Group Search: Discover groups with Allow Offers enabled. Open a card to Offer to Group.

    Find Active Users and Influencers

    Find Active Users and Influencers:

    • Enter Name for specific users.
    • Use Content Type (e.g., Gaming, Fitness, Fashion).
    • Filter by Location or choose a Platform.
    • Use Previous/Next to browse more results.
