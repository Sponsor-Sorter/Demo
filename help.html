    üì¨ How do sponsorships work?

    Sponsors send proposals through the platform. Once a sponsee accepts the offer, deliverables are agreed upon and tracked through our integrated workflow system.

    üí∞ When do I get paid?

    üí∏ How Do Refunds Work?

    If you cancel a sponsorship offer before it is accepted by the creator, the amount you paid will be refunded directly to your Sponsor Sorter wallet.

    Cancelled funds are instantly credited to your wallet. You can view your wallet balance at the top of your sponsor dashboard.

    How to Refund:


      Go to your Sponsor Dashboard

      Find your active offers and click Cancel Offer on the offer you wish to cancel.

      After confirmation, the offer will be marked cancelled and the offer amount refunded to your wallet.
    Note: Offers can only be refunded/cancelled before they are accepted by a creator.

    üëõ Understanding Your Sponsor Sorter Wallet

    Your wallet is a secure virtual balance within Sponsor Sorter. It holds:


      Refunds from cancelled offers

      Deposited or transferred funds
    How to Use Your Wallet:

      Request a Withdrawal: Click the wallet withdraw icon (💳) on your dashboard to transfer funds to your PayPal or bank account. Admin approval is required for withdrawals.

      Pay for New Offers: Funds in your wallet can be used for new sponsorship offers on the platform.
    Available wallet balance is always shown in green.
    If you have a pending withdrawal, the wallet balance will show in orange until your request is processed.

    For more details, contact support@sponsorsorter.com
