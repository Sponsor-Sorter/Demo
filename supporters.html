    Supporter FAQ
    When do I get my badge and free months?

    All badges and subscription time are applied when Sponsor Sorter’s full version launches. You’ll receive an email with instructions and everything will be tied to your Sponsor Sorter account.

    Do I need an account to become a supporter?

    We recommend creating a Sponsor Sorter account with the same email you use to donate so we can automatically attach your rewards. If you don’t have one yet, you can still support now and create your account later.

    Is this a one-time contribution?

    Yes. This page is for one-time launch support, not a recurring subscription. Your free months will start when the platform launches.

    How are payments processed?

    Donations are handled by our payment partners (like Stripe or PayPal). We never store your full card details directly.
